ABOUT US

MORE THAN A GYM.
A MOVEMENT.

Barbellhouse is Bengaluru's premier destination for serious lifters and athletes. Founded with a passion for Olympic weightlifting and functional fitness, we provide world-class equipment, expert coaching, and an unmatched training environment.

Located in RR Nagar, we specialize in weightlifting, crosslifting, and strength & conditioning. Whether you're a beginner learning your first snatch or a competitive athlete chasing a national record, Barbellhouse is where champions are made.
